jSlider.options.width Property

Below are demonstrations of the effects of different values for jSlider's options' width property.

1. Full Container Width

var 	containerOne = $('div#jSliderOne'),
	imagesOne = [
		'../../test_images/5.jpg',
		'../../test_images/6.jpg',
		'../../test_images/7.jpg',
		'../../test_images/8.jpg',
		'../../test_images/9.jpg'],
	optionsOne = { width: '100%' },

sliderOne = $.jSlider(containerOne, imagesOne, optionsOne);
